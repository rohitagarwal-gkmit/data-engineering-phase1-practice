Origin of the Word "Bluetooth"

It is said that in ancient days, a Scandinavian king had stained his teeth blue after eating a lot of blueberries, hence the word, Bluetooth.

How Bluetooth Speakers Technology Works

When two devices receive signals without docking to each other, then we say that Bluetooth is at work. Those bluetooth speakers devices need not be identical in nature. So, a laptop can connect using Bluetooth with a mobile, and vice versa. Likewise, a pair of speakers can catch and transmit signals with a remote television set or an audio system. The two devices must match their codes for the Bluetooth technology to work. When the two sets of codes match, we say that the two devices have paired.

Brands of Bluetooth Speakers

iBall, Zoook, Bose, JBL Pulse and Zaazz are some of the brands that are available in the market. You can check a large number of other brands as well by visiting Snapdeal's online shopping site.

These bluetooth speakers may have slots for memory cards and USB ports. Having a memory card enhances the choice of music, as one does not have to depend on the presence of an audio system.

The speakers come in various colours. A lot of people prefer these speakers in black and place them discreetly to enjoy a steady stream of music.

You may also have speakers that have a Wi-Fi connection, have LED lights and NFC and also are splash proof. Bluetooth speakers are quite versatile in performance.

Variety of Shapes to Choose From

These speakers come in some novel shapes. Gone are the days when speakers used to be only rectangular or circular in form. You can have egghead shaped speakers as well. The size also varies, and it is not uncommon to see speakers that beam out high decibel music, having tiny sizes.

The music output also varies from 10 watts to as high as 240 W. However, the PMPO of your Bluetooth speaker must match the sound output of your music or TV console. You can enjoy your music if you have a good anti-echo system in your house.
